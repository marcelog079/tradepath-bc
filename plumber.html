Electrician

Red Seal High Demand 4-Year Apprenticeship ITA Certified

Electricians are the backbone of BC's construction and infrastructure boom. From residential wiring to industrial automation and EV charging networks — demand has never been higher and it's only growing.

— Certification Path

Your Road To
Red Seal.

StartDay 1

Find a Sponsor Employer

Register with Skilled Trades BC and secure an employer willing to take you on as an apprentice. You'll earn wages from day one — no need to front tuition costs.

Yr 1~1800 hrs

1st Year Apprentice

On-the-job training combined with in-school technical training (6–8 weeks/year at a BC institution). Learn fundamentals: wiring, panels, code, safety.

Yr 2~3600 hrs

2nd Year Apprentice

More complex installations. Commercial and industrial exposure. Your hourly rate increases with each level.

Yr 3~5400 hrs

3rd Year Apprentice

Advanced systems, motor controls, and low-voltage work. You're now a highly capable tradesperson mid-path.

Yr 4~7200 hrs

4th Year + Certificate of Qualification

Complete your final in-school period and pass the Certificate of Qualification (CofQ) exam. You are now a Journeyperson Electrician in BC.

RedSeal

Interprovincial Red Seal Exam

Optional but highly recommended. Pass the Red Seal exam and your certification is recognized in every province in Canada. No re-certification if you move.
